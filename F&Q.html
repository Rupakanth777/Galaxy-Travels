Your Questions, Our Answers

What are your operating hours?

Our store is open from 9 AM to 6 PM, Monday through Saturday. We understand the importance of accessibility and strive to be available during convenient hours for our customers. Please note that we are closed on Sundays and public holidays to allow our staff to rest and rejuvenate.


How can I book a service

Booking a service with us is straightforward and user-friendly. You can book directly through our website by navigating to the "Bookings" section. Alternatively, if you prefer a more personal touch, you can call us directly at +91 7894561220. Our team is always ready to assist you in scheduling your appointment at your convenience.


What payment methods do you accept?

We offer several convenient payment methods to suit your needs. For online payments, you can easily transfer funds through the contact number provided. If you prefer paying by cash, you can hand it over to the driver upon delivery. For those who prefer using a card, you can visit our office to complete the transaction. We strive to make the payment process as seamless as possible for our valued customers.


Can I reschedule my booking

Absolutely! We understand that plans can change. You can reschedule your booking up to 24 hours in advance by contacting our support team at +91 7894561220. We will do our best to accommodate your new preferred time.


Where are you located?

You can find us at Shop Number 3, Near Bustand, Kodaikanal, Tamil Nadu. Our store is centrally located to serve the community effectively, making it easy for you to drop by for all your needs.
