KODAIKANAL - PRINCESS OF HILLS






Kodaikanal, often called the “Princess of Hill Stations,” is a serene hill station in Tamil Nadu, India, established in 1845. Nestled at an altitude of around 7,000 feet in the Palani Hills, it offers a cool climate and picturesque landscapes. Key attractions include the man-made Kodaikanal Lake, Coaker’s Walk, and the Kurinji Andavar Temple2. It’s a popular destination for nature lovers and honeymooners alike.
