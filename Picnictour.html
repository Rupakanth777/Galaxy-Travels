KODAIKANAL FULL DAY - PICNIC TOUR PACKAGE


In this package, enjoy a delightful Picnic Tour from 11 am to 5 pm, perfect for families and friends looking to explore the stunning landscapes of Kodaikanal. Your adventure begins at the charming La Saleth Church, known for its beautiful architecture and serene atmosphere. From there, we’ll head to the fascinating Lion Cave, a unique natural formation that offers both adventure and awe. Next, take in the breathtaking views at Dolphin Nose, where you can capture the stunning vistas of the valleys below. Feel the refreshing mist at Vattakanal Falls, a hidden gem where you can relax and soak in nature's beauty. As we continue, you’ll be mesmerized by the Mountain View, showcasing the majestic hills that Kodaikanal is famous for. Don’t miss the opportunity to see the 500-Year-Old Tree, a testament to nature’s endurance, and then enjoy the soothing sights and sounds of Bearshola Falls. Finally, we’ll conclude our tour at Echo Rock, where you can experience the fun of sound echoes in this picturesque setting. Throughout the day, our knowledgeable guide will provide insights and stories about each location, ensuring a memorable experience for all. Book this package to create lasting memories amidst Kodaikanal’s natural wonders!
